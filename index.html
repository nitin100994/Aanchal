About Us

Aanchal foundation is a charitable trust registered under Act certification number (XX). Aachal trust is a tribute to struggles of our forefathers who struggled on the face of adversitites to support their family, friends and community.

Aachal trust remind us of one of the struggles and commitant by Shri Bhim Dutt joshi and their brothers to dedicate his life for his family, friends and community.

Shri Bhim Dutt joshi, popularly know as "guru-ji" was a generous human-being, a furious soldier, a life-long learner, who comitted himself to the cause of other. He along with his wife Shri Sumitra devi guided family children toward the path of properity and took many countless initiatives to help others from Pahadi community. most of second-young generations of pahadis who now enjoyed living a comfortable lives in citites, can't imagine what it would be like to grow our own crops, water, fire or, worst of all, living with limited financial resources and huge responsibilitites towards their children, family, friends and communitites.achal foundation is a charitable trust registered under Act certification number (XX). "Guruji" and his struggles remind us that path to prosperity is neither automatic nor inevitable. Every step requires sacrifice, suffering, and struggle; the tireless exertions and passionate concern of dedicated individuals is what makes it possible.

This very "Aachal Foundation" is an effort by his family members to keep his values alive and to carry forward his legacy by helping the underprivileged sections of society.

Our Focus

Education

Enabling children with school uniforms, stationaries and books.

Financial aid to the children coming from financially and socially weaker sections of the society.

Maintainance and building school infrastructure such as toilets, school benches, school libraries etc.

Conduct educational and vocational webinars/seminars for 10th and 12th passout adolescents children.

Community Health

Conservation of natural resources with special focus to "WATER conservation".

Conduct various vocational tranings such as bee harvesting, animal harvesting, and innovative irrigation techniques.

Work pro-actively to spread awareness about social welfare schemes and security schemes funded by Central and State governments.

Education people and prepare them for disaster management.

Our motto is "Prevention is better than Cure" and therefore, we continue to promote and
support healthly lifestyle and eating habbits, better sanitation and hygiene.
